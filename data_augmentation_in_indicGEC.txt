Two types of augmentation
1. one at character level
2. one at word level
 Types of Augmentation
 1. Swap
 2. Insert
 3. Delete
 
 I am studying in Surat .
 1. Word Level
 1.1 Delete - I studying in Surat .
 1.2 Swap - Studying I am i Surat .
 1.3 Insert - I am man studying in Surat .
 
 2. Character Level
 2.1 Delete - I am studing in Surat .
 2.2 Swap - I ma studying in Surat .
 2.3 Insert - Ia am studying in Surat .
